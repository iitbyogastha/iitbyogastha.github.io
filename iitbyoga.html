Rules for Yoga Championship

Rules (please read carefully):
  1. The participant needs to perform a total of five asanas.
  2. Four asanas will be chosen randomly by the judges from your age group list.
  3. The participant is free to choose one asana (need not be from their list) to improve his / her competitive edge.
  4. The participant will be judged based on posture, stability and grace.
  5. The points given by the panel of judges will be used for ranking the participants.
Additional notes:
  1. Participant needs to carry a proof of date of birth (such as birth certificate).
  2. Shorts for boys and costumes for girls are mandatory.
  3. Spot entry is NOT allowed.
  4. IIT Bombay is a restricted area. Please carry the proof of registration and identity card for entering the premises.
  5. Prize distribution at 12:00 Noon for Group 1 & 2.
  6. Prize distribution at 5:00 PM for Group 3 & 4.
  7. No Refund / Cancellation / transfer policy after the registration.
