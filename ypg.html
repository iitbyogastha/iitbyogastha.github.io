Rules for Yog Pratiyogita

  1. The competition is free of cost. Any IIT Bombay resident may participate. ID proof must be carried at the time of the competition. (*Containing name and address)
  2. Categories for male and female are separate, and for each there are 3 age groups: 14-24, 25-35 and 36 and above.
  3. Each participant will select 4 asanas to perform out of the 10 choices in his or her group. The total time allotted to one person is 3 minutes, during which all 4 asanas are to be demonstrated. There is no time limit on the performance of individual asanas.
  4. The participant must hold each asana for 8-10 seconds.(*Unless informed otherwise)
  5. For asanas that can be performed in two ways, i.e., from the ‘left’ and ‘right’ sides of the body (e.g.- Ardha Matsyendrasana, Akarna Dhanurasana etc), participants need to perform them from one side only.
  6. There will be gold, silver and bronze medals for the three winners in each category (male and female), in each age group. The decision of the judges is final and binding.
